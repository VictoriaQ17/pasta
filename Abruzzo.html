Abruzzo

Abruzzo is known for its lush national parks and untouched mountains.

Abruzzi cuisine is known for using lots of lamb and chilli.

Arrosticini

Quite simply arrosticini is skewered lamb with herbs- a beautifully simple dish.

Ingredients include:

  • Diced lamb
  • Rosemary
  • Black pepper
